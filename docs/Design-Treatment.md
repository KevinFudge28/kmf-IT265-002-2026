## Core Gameplay Mechanics

### Primary Mechanics
<!-- 
The core gameplay mechanics are:
Turn-based movement by using dice rolls (1d4)
Hidden traps that are randomly placed on treasure spots
Searching for treasure and items that will support the hero
Combat from dice rolls (1d6)
Using your items (potion, sword, sheild) strategically
Strategically planning your escape and moves.

All of these mechanics make the player think strategically and makes the players try and balance risks and rewards throught the game.
-->

### Goals and Challenges
<!-- 
Each player has different things that they are trying to achieve. 
The hero heros goal is to collect all the treasure and escape from one of the corners of the board or they can defeat the monster.
The monsters goal is to defeat the hero before they escape.
Some of the challenges the players will face are hidden traps, deciding when to run or fight, using your potions strategically, managing your health, and predicting where your opponent will go.
These challenges drive player engagement by making the player think strategically in order to have a chance at defeating their opponent.
-->

### Progression
<!-- 
As the game contiunes the tension increases because the hero is collecting treasure and items to help them win, while the monster gets more aggressive trying to eliminate the hero. 
The itmes the hero can get are a sword (increases the next attacks damage), sheild (reduces the amount of damage taken on the next attack), and potion that heals the hero 5HP.
The hero has to be careful to avoid traps as well. Both players adopt to different strategies as the game contiunes. 
-->

### Game Rules
<!-- 
Players take turns
Each player gets two actions per turn, they can attack the other player or move around the board
Hero can use an action to search for items/treaure
Hero gets a bonus action that can be used for potions
Movement is only up, down, let, and right 
Attacking is allowed only if both players are on the same space.
The hero must collect both treasure items before they can escape
-->

4. Hero lands on a treasure spot but its actually a trap and they lose health
5. The monster is moving closer to the hero
6. Hero lands on an actual treasure spot and gets a sword
7. Both player end up on the same tile and enter combat
8. Hero defeats the monster or escapes from one of the coroners of the board
